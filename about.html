About Tonya

A celebration of creativity, nature, and simple joys.

A Lifestyle of Creativity and Connection

Positively Tonya is a lifestyle brand built around the passions of Tonya herself—a creative soul who finds joy in art, cooking, and birding. With a deep love for nature and the simple things in life, Tonya seeks to inspire others to embrace the beauty of the everyday.

Beyond her creative pursuits, Tonya offers professional voice-over services, bringing warmth and authenticity to every project. Her voice carries the same passion she pours into her art, recipes, and time spent observing the birds in her backyard.

Through this site, Tonya shares her love for quality products—items that reflect her commitment to craftsmanship and a meaningful connection to the outdoors. Whether it’s a hand-painted sketch, a thoughtfully prepared meal, or the quiet thrill of spotting a rare bird, Positively Tonya is about celebrating life’s simple pleasures and inviting you to do the same.
